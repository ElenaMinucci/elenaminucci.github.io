About Me

I model differences in human behaviour to build personalised AI.

I am an interdisciplinary PhD candidate and teaching assistant at the University of Glasgow, supervised by Prof Simone Stumpf and Dr Martin Lages. I am currently affiliated with the UKRI Social AI Centre for Doctoral Training and the GIST research section. My PhD work investigates personalising persuasive AI to help people adopt sustainable behaviours. I am working on a method to steer AI-generated language towards a target persuasive style derived from each user's individual linguistic patterns. My broader research interests include computational modelling of verbal and non-verbal human behaviour, intelligent conversational interfaces, methods for automatic user profiling, and personality recognition from text.

I have expertise in: User Modelling · Human-AI Interaction · Natural Language Processing · LLMs · Psycholinguistics · Computational Persuasion
